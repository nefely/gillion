Meet Gillion

Gillion Apes World presents a collection of 6110 Apes, divided into 3 clans and 4 classes, which add up to more than 150 unique attributes.

Gillion Apes are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain and hosted on IPFS.

Our goal is to create a complete ecosystem.

Based on this world, we develop our token, which will be the currency for our Play to Earn game, where each participant has the opportunity to have fun and exchange resources.

Each of our 3D avatars is now ready to be integrated into Metaverse.

Join a continuously growing community with exponentially increasing benefits and utilities. Let's build the future of entertainment together!

Clans

Gillion’s sentient beings split into three clans, each calling a different place their homeland.

Magdar

More

Magdar

Emblem:
Chief: Throg
Location: Tol Madar continent
An ancient clan, relishing power, endurance, wrath, and cunning. A true clan of warriors and hunters who knew no pity or weakness.
Chief’s right hand, general Krog, personally led the training of future warriors.
Cruel conditions, where only the strongest and most enduring could survive.
General Hun, the chief’s left hand, personally assigned each Magdari to his military role.

Renegades

More

Renegades

Emblem:
Location: Oxygen island.
An independent clan who bows to no chief. United by a common goal, each member had a say in the matters.
Despite the meager numbers, just 100 of chosen warriors, each of them was free.
Unshackled from the boundaries. Sharing knowledge and skills, they achieved an astounding level of progress.
They scattered across Gillion, tasked by finding new associates, secretly converting more and more apes to their cause. All this to end the eternal chaos and its consequences.

Aritas

More

Aritas

Emblem:
Chief: Baindrall
Location: Erator continent
Wisdom, intelligence, and magic are dominant within this clan.
A clan free from dictatorship where each is free to choose their trade.
Supreme Mage Karigas trained young acolytes. A legendary hero who survived countless wars.
Supreme Sage Tris taught the Eratorians who chose the sage path.

Warrior

Relies on strength, massive armor, and weapons fit for their brutal nature.

Type - Melee | Tank

Features - Damage blocking | Endurance | Accumulating strength through damage

Indicators - Anger | Aggression | Rage

Armor - Plate

Weapons - Sword | Axe | Shield

Talents - thanks to the massive armor and a shield, the Warrior can endure numerous attacks. Each attack only increases the rage brewing inside. Once the Warrior is close to the target, the rage is released, annihilating the enemy at once.

Hunter

Quick and cunning. A master of traps, Hunter plays with the victim without mercy, enjoying the suffering once the trap is activated.

Type - Melee | Ranged

Features - Animal taming | Traps | Camouflage

Indicators - Cunning | Focus | Speed

Armor - Cloth/ Leather

Weapons - Bow | Firearm | Polearms

Talents - thanks to light armor, the Hunter can hide easily, blending in with nature. Those lucky souls who have managed to survive the traps can brace for a deadly ranged attack!

Mage

High intelligence allows the Mage to control powerful magic that no ordinary person can tame and channel.

Type - Ranged

Features - Ranged magic | Area of Effect | Crowd control

Indicators - Frost magic | Nature magic | Fire magic

Armor - Plate

Weapons - Staff | Rod

Talents - massive armor protects the Mage from unexpected attacks. At the same time, magic enables them to attack the enemy at a great distance, minimizing the threat and allowing them to attack multiple hostiles simultaneously.

Monk

Controls the course of events by merging with the environment. Can easily read into the opponent’s plans.

Type - Melee | Healer

Features - Teleportation | Attack dodge | Heal

Indicators - Nature energy | Tranquility | Balance

Armor - Cloth

Weapons - Staff | Book

Talents - by merging with the environment, the Sage draws from “Anima” of nature, transforming it into energy clumps then used to attack or to heal self or an ally. Light armor allows easy maneuvering among enemies.

FAQ

01

WHAT IS GILLION APES?

It’s a unique NFT collection of 6110 3D art pieces with utilities for the Gillion Apes Ecosystem. There are more than 150 unique hand-drawn attributes.

02

GILLION APES NFT MINTING

Minting will take place exclusively on our website. We will share the exact date and time on Discord.

03

IS THERE A WHITELIST?

Yes, we have a WL. Active members can earn spots on our Discord.

04

HOW CAN I USE MY NFT?

Beyond the Gillion Apes collectibles, you can use the NFTs as an avatar in our Play to Earn Game. Also Gillion Apes avatars will be integrated into other Metaverses.

05

WHAT IS METAMASK?

Metamask is a crypto wallet that stores your Ethereum, which you need to purchase and mint Gillion Apes NFT.

06

WHAT BLOCKCHAIN IS IT ON?

The Gillion Apes collection uses the ERC-721 token on the Ethereum Blockchain. File hosting is IPFS.

06

WHERE CAN I SEE MY NFT?

Once you successfully mint Gillion Apes NFT, you can view it by connecting your wallet to OpenSea.

07

ARE THERE ANY ROYALTY FEES?

The royalty fees are fixed at 7%. A portion of those royalties will be used for Marketing to help the Gillon Apes community grow. Our team is very committed to the long-term success of this project. We are here to stay!

08

HOW DO I CONTACT THE TEAM?

You can either join our discord and ask any questions you have or send us an email at contact@gillionapes.com.
